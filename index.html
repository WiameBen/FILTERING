Climatic Indices

The climatic indices used for filtering are:

FD: Number of frost days
ID: Number of icing days
SU: Number of summer days
DTmax35: Days with Tmax more than 35
TR: Number of tropical nights
R5mm: Days with rain at least 5 mm

R10mm: Days with rain at least 10 mm
SDII: Simple precipitation intensity index
Rx5day: Maximum 5-day precipitation
PRECtot: Total precipitation
DTR: Daily temperature range
CDD: Consecutive Dry Days
CWD: Consecutive Wet Days
